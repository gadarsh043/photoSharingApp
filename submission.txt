Story number and name: 1 - Extend user profile detail with usage
Story points: 3
Brief description to see the story in action:
The user profile now displays the following:
Most recently uploaded photo (with a small thumbnail and the upload date).
The photo with the most comments (including a small thumbnail and the comment count).
Clicking on either photo switches to the user photos view, where the specific photo and its comments are visible (works with advance features also).

---

Story number and name: 3 - Visibility control of photos
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action:
When uploading a photo, users can specify a sharing list to control who can view the photo.
If no sharing list is provided, the photo is visible to everyone.
If the sharing list is empty, only the photo owner can view it.
(This is reflected in the displayed photos and photo counts, ensuring privacy settings are honored.)

---

Story number and name: 4 - Activity feed
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action:
The Activity Feed shows the 5 most recent activities for the current user, such as photo uploads, comments, and login/logout events.
(This feed dynamically updates to display new activities as they occur).
(This also ensures privacy settings are honored).

---

Story number and name: 6 - Photo "like" votes
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action:
Users can like or unlike photos.
The total like count is displayed alongside each photo.
Likes are updated in real-time to reflect changes by the current user.

---

Story number and name: 8 - Sidebar list marks users with new activity
Story points: 3
Brief description to see the story in action:
The sidebar shows all users with their most recent activities, such as photo uploads, comments, and login/logout events.
For photo uploads, comments, a thumbnail of the photo is displayed. The sidebar updates dynamically when new activities occur.
(We are using websocket to achieve this)
